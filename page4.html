Flowers

A odd thing about me is that I like flowers a lot, my favourite flower is the "Black Petunia."

Petunia

A Petunia is genus of 20 species of South American origin. This derives from the french word, "petun" which means tobacoo.

Black Petunia

Black petunias are more visually appealing than other foliage because of their dramatic color. They are relatively easy to grow, and they last longer than other flowers.
